Reliance Jio Rs 222 Recharge Plan Offers Unlimited Data, 30 Days Validity

By
Venkatesh Gorantla
-
Published On: December 7, 2022
JOIN US

Reliance Jio has introduced a new recharge plan in India. The leading telecom operator launched by billionaire Mukesh Ambani has announced a Rs 222 recharge plan for prepaid customers. The new Reliance Jio plan has been launched (via) specially for the ongoing FIFA World Cup. It is a 4G data-only voucher, which means that Jio customers need to have an active plan to avail benefits of the new Rs 222 plan.

The plan is suitable for those who consume more 4G data than the capped limit of up to 2GB per day. Jio is calling the Rs 222 recharge plan the ‘Football World Cup data pack’, which is rightly targeted towards those who stream the FIFA World Cup 2022 matches live on their smartphones. Let’s take a look at more details about the new Reliance Jio Rs 222 recharge plan validity, benefits and other details.

What Does the Reliance Jio Rs 222 Offer?

The new Reliance Jio Rs 222 plan is a recharge pack for those who want more data than the capped 2GB limit per day. The plan requires users to have an active base plan. Users can have any of the primary Reliance Jio recharge plans, which offer unlimited calling, voice calls and other benefits.

The Rs 222 plan comes with 30 days of validity. It provides 50GB of high-speed 4G data. The Rs 222 recharge voucher’s data is consumed only after the primary plan’s 2GB data gets exhausted. Once the 50GB data is consumed, the internet speed will be reduced to 64 Kbps.

Also read: Jio Recharge Plans and Offers [December 2022]: Jio New Prepaid Recharge Plan Price List with Talktime, Validity, 4G Data Benefits

As mentioned above, the plan is a 4G data voucher. This means it does not offer the benefits of voice calls, SMS or any free subscription to OTT services that the primary packs are likely to provide.

Users who wish to subscribe to the add-on recharge plan can do so by going to the MyJio app or the Jio website. The plan is called the ‘Football World Cup data pack’ and is available in the recharge section of the app and website.

Also read: Reliance Jio Silently Discontinues Prepaid Recharge Plans Offering Disney+ Hotstar Mobile Subscription

Since the plan has been titled ‘Football World Cup data pack’, it is possible that Jio will discontinue the recharge pack once the FIFA World Cup 2022 final is over. The company has not revealed any details about the same.
